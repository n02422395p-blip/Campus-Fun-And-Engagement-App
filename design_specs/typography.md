# Typography System

## Font Family
- **Primary**: Poppins (Download from Google Fonts)
- **Secondary**: Inter (Fallback)

## Font Sizes & Weights
| Element | Size | Weight | Line Height |
|---------|------|--------|-------------|
| Headline 1 | 32px | Bold | 40px |
| Headline 2 | 24px | Bold | 32px |
| Title | 20px | Semi-bold | 28px |
| Body Large | 16px | Regular | 24px |
| Body | 14px | Regular | 20px |
| Caption | 12px | Regular | 16px |
| Button | 16px | Bold | 24px |
